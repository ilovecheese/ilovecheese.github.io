First Tinker With SDR

Feb 12th, 2014

Well I have been interested in HAM radio for years now and have been talking about getting hardware and my liscense. Recently I read sniffing-and-decoding-nrf24l01. Figured this would get me back motivated for getting my license. I also found a cool application for tracking airplanes on Hak5. Have not tried the airplane one yet. The dongle that I ordered is not good quality, it requires me to hold pressure on the USB or it disconnects. I have a different one in route now. I also bought a antenna. More details on that later. If it does not work well I’ll make my own. I have not had the best of luck running SDR# in mono in Linux yet (think it’s from the random kernel detaches from the crappy design). Looking after I get licensed about getting a upconverter and just using a SDR setup on the HAM bands.

Tootles for now!
